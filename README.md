# 📖 Overview

**Emotional AI Investment Advisor** is an AI-powered conversational platform designed to assist users in making smarter investment decisions by understanding both their **financial goals** and **emotional behavior**.

Traditional investment advisory platforms rely mainly on financial metrics. This project takes a different approach by incorporating **behavioral finance principles**, enabling the system to recognize emotions such as fear, greed, anxiety, and overconfidence before providing personalized investment recommendations.

The platform aims to improve financial decision-making by reducing emotionally driven investment mistakes and encouraging long-term wealth creation.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Many investors make irrational investment decisions because of emotions during market fluctuations.

Common challenges include:

- Panic selling during market crashes
- Greed during bull markets
- Lack of financial planning
- Poor risk management
- No personalized investment guidance

This project addresses these issues by combining AI-based conversations with financial planning and behavioral analysis.
